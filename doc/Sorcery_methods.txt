List of publicly available Sorcery methods:

Sorcery.require(dependables,callback)
	similar to RequireJS's require()
Sorcery.define(dependable,callback)
	similar to RequireJS's define()

Sorcery.require_environment(environment)
	ensures specific environment - Sorcery.ENVIRONMENT_CLI or Sorcery.ENVIRONMENT_WEB
	throws error if environment is wrong
	needed for cli-only and web-only services
